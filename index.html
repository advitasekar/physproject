Navigating Climate Change

Advita Sekar

I am a Senior Data Scientist at ClimaPredict Innovations, a global research institution that makes predictive models about the impact of climate change. Through my academic journey, I have always been interested in machine learning and artificial intelligence, and this position gave me the opportunity to utilize my skills to make a difference and spread awareness about the disastrous consequences of global warming. At this institution, we build models with high accuracy to predict the impacts of climate change on agriculture, infrastructure, marine life, and other sectors. Our models process datasets from satellites, previous weather records, and climate sensors and it also has access to the internet, feeding it with various resources about trends associated with the climate. Using this information, it forecasts extreme weather events, allowing preparation by warning people about the potential consequences of climate change. This information is critical for communities to begin strategizing about how to mitigate climate change effectively. My role at this company is to maintain the accuracy of the models, make the required updates to the models, and to collaborate with organizations fighting climate change in order to ensure our models are highly accurate and practical.
